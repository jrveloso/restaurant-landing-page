About Us

Coco's is a friendly, neighborhood bar and grill, where we serve classic American comfort food and drinks for all of our neighbors to enjoy. All of our ingredients are locally sourced and we welcome all people with all sorts of dietary restrictions. Drop by for food, booze, and a good time.
